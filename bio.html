A brief history in words:

Angela Colley is an accomplished journalist with a decade-long track record of helping people lead easier, more informed lives. She currently writes investigative news, feature stories and blog posts for The Street Network, Money Talks News and Dealnews. Her work has appeared on other popular publications like MSN Money, Yahoo! Finance, CNBC, Business Insider and The Huffington Post.

Angela has also been praised by publications like Bankrate and Kiplinger and interviewed as a personal finance expert on KGO Radio for San Francisco’s Consumer Talk program.

Before freelancing, she served as Editor-in-Chief for the Solana Breeze, a publication serving the mortgage industry. She was awarded Yahoo!'s first Spotlight Contributor Award.
